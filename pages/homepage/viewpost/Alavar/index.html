Alavar Seafood Restaurant

Posted on May 5, 2024 | By Zamchiver | 173 Don Alfaro St, Zamboanga, 7000 Zamboanga del Sur

Food & Drink Restaurants Tourist Spot

An iconic Zamboanga restaurant widely celebrated for its unique and flavorful Curacha (spanner crab) served with a rich, savory Alavar sauce made from coconut milk, crab roe, and secret spices. A must-try culinary destination for visitors.
